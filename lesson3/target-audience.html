Target Audience

The target audience for this website will be mostly for those who are entrepreneurs working toward growing their business. This site is also aimed toward those who might be interested in joining the Chamber of Commerce. We hope that all people may be able to find some use in our website to increase the positive impact that our Chamber of Commerce may have on the surrounding businesses.

Scenarios

  1. A person comes to the site in search of local businesses and is able to find what he/she is looking for
  2. A person is able to find a page that allows them to join the Chamber of Commerce
  3. A person comes to the site searching for fun recreational activities and is able to find them.
  4. A person is looking for a way to contact the Head Chairperson of the Chamber of Commerce and is able to do so.
  5. A person who has joined the Chamber of Commerce is looking for special events for the members and is able to do so.
