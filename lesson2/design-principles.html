Hick's Law

Hick's Law is a law based around decision making. It explains that in order to get a faster decision-making process, you must first reduce the number of stimuli present. One example that I thought of was the Apple website. They have reduced the stimuli on their front page so that it has become VERY easy for the user to quickly decide on which one of Apple's new products the user would want to look into.
